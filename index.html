First time?

Going to a hairstylist for the first time can be a bit nerve wracking. I want you to know that from the moment you fall on this page, to the moment you leave my chair, you and your hair will be taken care of.

I can't wait to meet you!

The experience starts when you use the online booking request below. Your appointment request will be received and you will then receive a confirmation within 24 hours to confirm your appointment.

Next is when you arrive at the studio. Come inside and take a seat, and I will bring you to my chair! At this time, we will go through about a 15 minute consultation process. We will plan out, not just your first visit, but a long term plan to ensure all your hair goals are met.

If you're here for a color, a formula will be custom blended for you and recorded. This gives me a history of what we do together which will create consistent results and seamless transitions for any fun future changes! If you are here for a cut, I will create a shape that is perfect for your features, lifestyle and overall hair goals.

We'll wrap up the visit by sharing my recommendations for at-home care and set you up with your next visit!

Cancellation Etiquette

Please reschedule or cancel at least 24 hours before your appointment begins to avoid any late cancellation fee. If less than 24 hours notice is given you may be charged 50% of the total value of the appointment you have missed. Thank you!
